CITIES IN INDIA

Hyderabad

Famous Place: Charminar
Famous Food: Hyderabadi Biryani
Description: Hyderabad is known as the City of Pearls and is famous for its rich culture.


Mumbai

Famous Place: Gateway of India
Famous Food: Vada Pav
Description: Mumbai is the financial capital of India and home to Bollywood.


Delhi

Famous Place: Red Fort
Famous Food: Chole Bhature
Description: Delhi is the capital city of India and has many historical monuments.


Jaipur

Famous Place: Hawa Mahal
Famous Food: Dal Baati Churma
Description: Jaipur is known as the Pink City and attracts many tourists.


Kolkata

Famous Place: Victoria Memorial
Famous Food: Rasgulla
Description: Kolkata is famous for its culture, literature, and sweets.
